About Me

I am Muhammad Okasha, a passionate Frontend-focused Web Developer and aspiring Full-Stack (MERN) Developer. I specialize in building modern, responsive, and user-friendly web interfaces while continuously strengthening my backend skills to become a complete full-stack developer.

I have completed my Intermediate education and pursued professional web development training from Aptech, where I built a strong foundation in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Bootstrap, Tailwind CSS, and modern frontend practices. Alongside this, I am actively preparing for full-stack development using the MERN stack.

Frontend development is my primary strength, and I enjoy crafting engaging user experiences with clean code and modern design principles. At the same time, I am continuously learning backend technologies to build scalable, end-to-end web applications.

My career goal is to work professionally in the industry through a job, while also pursuing freelancing to gain real-world experience, expand my skill set, and deliver impactful digital solutions.
